To: Department of Defense (DoD) / Department of Homeland Security (DHS) / Department of Commerce (DOC) / National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA) / Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

Request:

Requesting any and all purchase, shipment, distribution, or sales data — including bulk procurement records — from 2000 to present related to solar eclipse viewing glasses or other protective eyewear marketed for astronomical viewing.

This includes:
	•	Federal, state, or local agency purchases of eclipse glasses for public distribution
	•	Contracts or agreements with private vendors, manufacturers, or distributors for eclipse glasses
	•	Aggregated or anonymized retail sales data obtained or requested by federal agencies regarding eclipse glasses before major solar eclipses
	•	Internal communications, reports, or contractor deliverables analyzing purchase patterns, geographic concentration, or demographic distribution of eclipse glasses buyers
	•	Use of eclipse glasses purchase or distribution data in crowd movement prediction, public gathering modeling, or environmental monitoring projects during eclipse events
	•	Any interagency or contractor collaborations linking eclipse glasses purchase data with human factors research, environmental exposure trials, or gravitational gradient testing
