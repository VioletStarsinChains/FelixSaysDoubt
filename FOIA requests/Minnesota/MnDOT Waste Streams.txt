To Minnesota Department of Transportation (MnDOT) / Minneapolis Public Works / Hennepin County Department of Transportation / Minnesota Pollution Control Agency (MPCA)

Request:

Requesting any and all documents, contracts, audits, or internal communications concerning the handling, disposal, and monitoring of waste materials from road, sidewalk, and related construction projects within the state of Minnesota, with particular attention to projects in the Minneapolis–Saint Paul metropolitan area, from 1990 to present.

This includes:
	•	Contracts with private waste haulers, landfills, or disposal facilities for construction debris, excavated soil, and demolition rubble
	•	Records of material manifests, showing source and destination of fill, soil, or debris removed during Minneapolis street or sidewalk projects
	•	Audits, inspector general reviews, or city/county reports addressing irregularities in waste volumes, disposal costs, or subcontractor practices
	•	Environmental monitoring data collected near road or sidewalk construction sites, including volatile organic compounds, amines (e.g., putrescine, cadaverine, skatole), or other decomposition-associated compounds
	•	Internal communications or policy memos regarding expedited or non-standard disposal of excavation material from city or state projects
	•	Risk assessments, environmental reviews, or MPCA documentation concerning potential health effects of volatile emissions from construction waste streams in residential neighborhoods

Keywords: construction waste, road debris, sidewalk excavation, waste haulers, disposal contracts, soil manifests, putrescine, cadaverine, skatole, volatile amines, MnDOT, MPCA, Minneapolis Public Works
