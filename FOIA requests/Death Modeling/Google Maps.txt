To Department of Transportation (DOT), Federal Highway Administration (FHWA), and Federal Trade Commission (FTC)

Request:

Requesting any and all documents, contracts, pilot program reports, or internal communications from 2008 to present concerning navigation application reliability and safety risks, including application failures, crashes, or service interruptions during active route guidance.

This includes:
	•	Records of studies or reports on the safety impact of navigation app crashes or failures while a vehicle is in motion
	•	Documentation of federal partnerships or research with providers such as Google Maps, Waze, and Apple Maps regarding app reliability, crash rates, and mitigation strategies
	•	Internal communications on consumer complaints, accident reports, or hazard analyses linked to navigation app failures
	•	Contracts or evaluations concerning federal oversight of app-based navigation reliability in relation to roadway safety and driver risk
	•	Oversight reports, audits, or ethical reviews regarding the safety implications of navigation app interruptions during active guidance

Keywords: navigation app, Google Maps, Waze, Apple Maps, reliability, application crash, service interruption, safety risk, DOT, FHWA, FTC
