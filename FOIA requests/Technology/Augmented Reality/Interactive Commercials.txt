FOIA – Interactive Advertising & Federal Contracts

To: Federal Trade Commission (FTC) / Federal Communications Commission (FCC) / Department of Commerce

Request:
Requesting any and all records, reports, contracts, or interagency communications from 2005 to present concerning interactive or gamified television advertising technologies, including methods for converting commercials into interactive network video games as described in related patents.

This includes:
	•	Contracts or pilot programs involving interactive or gamified advertising delivery systems
	•	Reports evaluating the effectiveness of interactive commercials, including user engagement data and demographic analysis
	•	Communications with broadcasters, advertising agencies, or technology vendors about integration of interactive game segments into television or streaming media
	•	Evaluations of consumer protection issues related to children’s exposure to interactive commercials framed as games

Keywords: interactive advertising, gamified commercials, FTC, FCC, behavioral advertising, streaming media ads, children’s advertising

FOIA (Spicy Angle – Interactive Commercials as Behavioral Futures Entrainment)

To: Department of Defense (DoD) / Department of Homeland Security (DHS) / Federal Communications Commission (FCC)

Request:
Requesting any and all records, contracts, reports, or internal analyses from 2005 to present concerning the use of interactive or gamified television commercials as behavioral entrainment tools and continuity theater mechanisms in behavioral futures markets.

This includes:
	•	Records describing interactive commercials used as experimental platforms for behavioral modeling and compliance testing
	•	Reports on the role of interactive game-ads in biosurveillance, including collection of biometric or demographic data during gameplay
	•	Communications with technology vendors or broadcasters regarding the use of gamified commercials in population-level simulations or continuity theater scenarios
	•	Analyses linking interactive advertising systems to behavioral futures markets, predictive modeling, or entrainment frameworks

Keywords: interactive commercials, gamified advertising, continuity theater, behavioral futures, biosurveillance, advertising entrainment, DoD, DHS, FCC
