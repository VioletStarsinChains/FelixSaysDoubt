FOIA – Porta Potty Procurement & Waste Processing

To: General Services Administration (GSA) / National Park Service (NPS) / U.S. Forest Service (USFS) / Environmental Protection Agency (EPA – Office of Resource Conservation and Recovery)

Request:
Requesting any and all procurement records, contracts, invoices, reports, and interagency communications from 1990 to present concerning federal agency contracting for portable sanitation services (porta potties, chemical toilets, or portable restroom facilities) in national parks, forests, and other federally managed public spaces.

This includes:
	•	Procurement and service contracts with private waste management vendors
	•	Reports on the collection, transport, and processing of waste from portable toilets on federal lands
	•	Communications regarding recycling, waste treatment, or reuse of byproducts collected from porta potties
	•	Audits or evaluations of porta potty service costs and disposal practices

Keywords: porta potty contracts, portable sanitation, chemical toilets, waste processing, NPS, USFS, GSA

FOIA (Spicy Angle – Metals Reclaimed from Porta Potty Waste)

To: Department of Defense (DoD – DARPA, USAMRDC) / Department of Energy (DOE – Critical Materials Institute) / Department of Homeland Security (DHS – S&T)

Request:
Requesting any and all records, contracts, pilots, reports, or internal analyses from 1990 to present concerning the recovery of strategic or industrial metals from human waste collected in portable sanitation units deployed in parks, festivals, trail systems, or other public spaces.

This includes:
	•	Records of partnerships with porta potty service providers for secondary recovery of metals (magnesium, copper, zinc, rare earths) from human excreta
	•	Contracts with vendors or labs studying chemical extraction processes for portable sanitation waste streams
	•	Reports describing field-scale recovery pilots or demonstrations involving porta potty waste as a source of critical materials
	•	Risk/benefit assessments of using porta potty collection infrastructure as covert material recovery pipelines
	•	Communications referencing “critical materials recovery,” “biosolids mining,” or equivalent terminology in connection with portable sanitation

Keywords: porta potty contracts, metals recovery, human waste reclamation, biosolids mining, critical materials, DARPA, DOE, DHS
