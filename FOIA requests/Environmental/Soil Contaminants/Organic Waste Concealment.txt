Generic State-Level Public Records Request Template

(for orchards + organic mortalities)

To [State Department of Agriculture] / [State Environmental Protection Agency or Pollution Control Agency] / [State Department of Natural Resources or Environmental Quality] / [State University Agricultural Experiment Station or Extension Service]

Request:

Pursuant to the [State Public Records Act / State Government Data Practices Act / applicable state statute], I am requesting any and all reports, inspection records, internal communications, or interagency memoranda concerning the burial, composting, or concealment of organic mortalities (including livestock or other carcasses) within or adjacent to orchard operations in [State], from 1990 to present.

This request includes, but is not limited to:
	•	Inspection reports or enforcement actions related to orchard operators using tree rows or orchard soils for disposal of animal mortalities or other organic waste
	•	Environmental impact assessments, soil sampling, or groundwater testing concerning contamination risks linked to buried organic matter in orchards
	•	Research proposals, extension bulletins, or grant-funded projects by [State University or Agricultural Experiment Station] regarding orchard soil management practices that involve carcass decomposition, mortality composting, or biomass recycling
	•	Interagency communications between [State Department of Agriculture], [Environmental/Pollution Control agency], and [DNR/DEQ] on the risks or regulatory status of using orchards for organic waste concealment, including carcasses
	•	Contractor, consultant, or industry reports evaluating the feasibility or risks of carcass burial/composting in orchard soils, and potential impacts on fruit safety or food supply chain integrity

Keywords: orchard, carcass disposal, burial, composting, livestock mortality, organic waste concealment, soil contamination, groundwater risk, [State Department of Agriculture], [State Environmental Agency], [State University Agricultural Experiment Station]
