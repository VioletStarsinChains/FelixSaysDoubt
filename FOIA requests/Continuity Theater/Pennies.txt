FOIA – Copper & Penny Production

To: U.S. Department of the Treasury / U.S. Mint

Request:
Requesting any and all records, reports, contracts, or interagency communications from 2000 to present concerning the sourcing, usage, and substitution of copper and zinc in the production of U.S. one-cent coins.

This includes:
	•	Records of copper procurement and allocation for penny production vs. other strategic applications
	•	Reports on the costs of penny minting and economic evaluations of discontinuing the coin
	•	Contracts with mining companies, refiners, or material suppliers for penny manufacturing
	•	Internal memoranda regarding policy decisions about maintaining or phasing out the penny

Keywords: U.S. Mint, penny production, copper sourcing, zinc coins, seigniorage, Treasury, copper scarcity, coinage policy
