FOIA – Attrition & Recruitment Data

To: Department of Justice (DOJ) / Bureau of Justice Statistics (BJS) / Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI – Uniform Crime Reports Division)

Request:
Requesting any and all records, reports, contracts, or interagency communications from 2019 to present concerning attrition, recruitment, and mortality rates among law enforcement officers in the United States.

This includes:
	•	Reports quantifying officer attrition during and after the Covid-19 pandemic
	•	Analyses of mortality among police compared to baseline civilian populations between 2019–2025
	•	Contracts or communications with universities, think tanks, or private contractors studying recruitment trends or staffing shortages
	•	Internal memoranda discussing discrepancies between reported expansion of police forces and underlying attrition data

Keywords: law enforcement attrition, police recruitment, officer mortality, Covid-19 impact, Bureau of Justice Statistics, FBI UCR, DOJ
