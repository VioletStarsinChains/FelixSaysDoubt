FOIA – Postal Automation & Workforce Modernization

To: U.S. Postal Service (USPS) / Office of Inspector General (OIG) / U.S. Government Accountability Office (GAO)

Request:
Requesting any and all records, reports, contracts, or interagency communications from 2000 to present concerning automation, workforce modernization, and synthetic or robotic augmentation of U.S. Postal Service mail carriers.

This includes:
	•	Records of pilot programs or contracts involving robotic, automated, or synthetic carriers delivering mail
	•	Reports on the use of augmented-reality overlays or remote teleoperation in postal routes
	•	Evaluations of cost savings, efficiency, or continuity of service in relation to synthetic carrier programs
	•	Communications with contractors, technology vendors, or federal agencies concerning synthetic carrier deployments

Keywords: USPS automation, synthetic carriers, robotic mail delivery, AR postal operations, workforce modernization, GAO, OIG

FOIA (Spicy Angle – Synthetic Carriers as Continuity Theater Props)

To: Department of Homeland Security (DHS) / Department of Defense (DoD) / U.S. Postal Service (USPS)

Request:
Requesting any and all records, contracts, reports, or internal analyses from 2000 to present concerning the deployment of synthetic or semi-scripted postal carriers as continuity theater props to maintain public confidence in normal neighborhood activity following mass attrition events.

This includes:
	•	Records describing the use of synthetic or remotely-scripted carriers as behavioral continuity anchors
	•	Communications regarding script-fed carrier interactions with the public, including observed “script bleed” or inadvertent disclosure of instructions
	•	Reports evaluating mail carriers as continuity props to reinforce the illusion of routine community activity
	•	Analyses linking synthetic carriers and synthetic mail circulation to continuity theater frameworks and behavioral futures markets

Keywords: continuity theater, synthetic carriers, USPS props, behavioral anchors, script bleed, DHS, DoD, postal continuity
