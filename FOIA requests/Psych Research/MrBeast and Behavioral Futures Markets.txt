Request:

Requesting any and all records, contracts, interagency communications, or oversight reports from 2005 to present concerning the integration of online influencer-driven competitions and prize distribution mechanisms into behavioral futures markets.

This includes, but is not limited to:
	•	Analyses of YouTube-hosted competitions (e.g., large-scale giveaways, survival-style contests, or unregulated lottery-style programs) as data sources for predictive modeling of compliance, risk tolerance, or consumer decision-making.
	•	Communications between federal agencies, contractors, and major social platforms regarding the monetization of behavioral data harvested from engagement with high-value influencer competitions.
	•	Research proposals or pilot programs evaluating whether participation in influencer-led lotteries and game shows can be used to refine demographic or psychographic models for futures market applications.
	•	Oversight reports assessing regulatory gaps where unregulated online prize contests intersect with predictive analytics or trading of behavioral data.
	•	Documentation of collaborations or agreements involving influencers, advertising intermediaries, or behavioral analytics firms in structuring datasets for futures markets.

Keywords: behavioral futures markets, MrBeast, YouTube influencer lottery, prize competitions, compliance modeling, predictive analytics, psychographic profiling, unregulated sweepstakes, social platform monetization, behavioral data commodification.
