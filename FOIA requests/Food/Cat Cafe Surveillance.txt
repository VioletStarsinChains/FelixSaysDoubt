To National Institutes of Health (NIH) / National Science Foundation (NSF) / Food and Drug Administration (FDA – Center for Food Safety and Applied Nutrition) / Department of Defense (DoD – DARPA / U.S. Army Research Office)

Request:

Requesting any and all contracts, research proposals, internal communications, or oversight reports concerning the use of animal cafés (including cat cafes in the United States) as sites for public health monitoring, behavioral observation, or environmental surveillance, from 2000 to present.

This includes:
	•	NIH- or NSF-funded studies involving animal cafes as structured environments for behavioral, psychological, or social research
	•	FDA or USDA communications regarding zoonotic disease surveillance, allergen exposure, or public health assessments in cat cafes and similar animal-based hospitality settings
	•	DoD or DARPA research proposals exploring animal cafés as testbeds for human-animal interaction, stress modulation, or ambient data collection
	•	Interagency memoranda, ethics board reviews, or contractor reports on animal cafes used as observational platforms in U.S. cities
	•	Incident reports or inspection findings documenting environmental or occupational health risks associated with cat cafes
