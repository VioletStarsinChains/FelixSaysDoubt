Request:

Pursuant to the Freedom of Information Act (5 U.S.C. § 552), I am requesting access to any and all records, from January 1, 2018 to present, regarding the inclusion of hemp-derived, synthetic, or semi-synthetic cannabinoid products in foods or beverages distributed in the United States.

This request includes but is not limited to:
	•	Regulatory communications (internal memoranda, emails, policy drafts) concerning cannabinoids (including but not limited to delta-8 THC, HHC, THC-O, THCP) in foods and beverages.
	•	Laboratory test results or enforcement actions regarding adulteration of foodservice items (sauces, syrups, honey packets, condiments) with cannabinoids.
	•	Consumer complaints or adverse event reports linked to ingestion of cannabinoids in foods or beverages not marketed as cannabinoid products.
	•	Contracts, permits, or licenses relating to foodservice distributors or manufacturers incorporating cannabinoids into supply-chain ingredients.
	•	Guidance documents, policy statements, or advisories regarding the use of cannabinoids in shelf-stable emulsions, flavorings, or condiments.
	•	Inter-agency communications between FDA, USDA, DEA, or state regulators regarding enforcement gaps or regulatory uncertainty over cannabinoids in foods and beverages.
