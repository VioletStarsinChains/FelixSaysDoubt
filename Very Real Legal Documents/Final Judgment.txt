FINAL JUDGMENT

Pursuant to the Court’s Order granting Plaintiff’s Motion for Summary Judgment, and there being no just reason for delay,

IT IS HEREBY ORDERED AND ADJUDGED that:
	1.	Judgment is entered in favor of Plaintiff and against Defendants.
	2.	Defendants’ classification of human beings as “non-person entities,” and the trading of such persons in behavioral futures markets, is declared unconstitutional, unlawful, and void ab initio.
	3.	Defendants are permanently enjoined from continuing such practices.
	4.	Defendants shall take immediate steps to delete all unlawfully obtained data and to restore housing, recognition, and other material relief to affected persons.
	5.	This Judgment is final and appealable.

SO ORDERED.
