INDICTMENT OUTLINE

COUNT I – Torture of a Minor (18 U.S.C. § 2340A)
Alleging Defendant knowingly subjected Plaintiff, a minor, to severe pain and suffering through covert biomedical implantation and psychological torture.

COUNT II – Conspiracy to Deprive Civil Rights (18 U.S.C. § 241)
Alleging Defendant conspired with contractors and government agents to deprive Plaintiff of constitutional rights, including bodily autonomy and due process.

COUNT III – Deprivation of Rights Under Color of Law (18 U.S.C. § 242)
Alleging Defendant, acting with and through government-connected entities, participated in the willful denial of Plaintiff’s rights.

COUNT IV – Sex Trafficking of a Minor by Force, Fraud, or Coercion (18 U.S.C. § 1591)
Alleging Defendant sold, transferred, or otherwise provided nonconsensual access to Plaintiff’s body via neurotechnology arrays for the purpose of sexual exploitation.

COUNT V – Healthcare Fraud (18 U.S.C. § 1347)
Alleging Defendant knowingly defrauded healthcare programs by concealing covert interventions, enabling billing fraud or misrepresentation of Plaintiff’s true medical condition.

COUNT VI – Computer Fraud and Abuse (18 U.S.C. § 1030)
Alleging Defendant knowingly authorized unauthorized access to computer systems/neurotechnology arrays in furtherance of exploitation.

COUNT VII – Child Abuse and Endangerment (State Analog)
Alleging Defendant knowingly exposed Plaintiff to physical harm, medical endangerment, and lifelong trauma as a minor.

⸻

FORFEITURE ALLEGATION
Pursuant to 18 U.S.C. §§ 981 and 982, the government seeks forfeiture of all assets derived from or traceable to the above offenses, including any funds, accounts, or property obtained by William Alton Clay as a result of the exploitation.
