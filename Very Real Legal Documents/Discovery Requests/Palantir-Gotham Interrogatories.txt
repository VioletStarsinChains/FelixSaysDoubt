Discovery Request List (Palantir / Gotham Context)

PLAINTIFF’S FIRST SET OF REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION

Plaintiff Kellyn Clay requests that Defendants produce the following documents and data, covering January 1, 2012 to present, relating to anomaly classification and the use of implant-derived data:

⸻

A. Model & Feature Documentation
	1.	All model cards, feature dictionaries, and technical documentation for anomaly detection models deployed in Gotham, Foundry, or successor platforms.
	2.	Any feature lineage graphs or data-flow diagrams showing implant-sourced telemetry or physiological streams.
	3.	Records of model training datasets, including source catalogs and feature provenance.

⸻

B. Source & Integration Catalogs
	4.	Complete data-source catalogs identifying inbound feeds, APIs, or SDKs labeled “implant,” “biosensor,” “telemetry,” “medical device,” or equivalents.
	5.	Vendor integration contracts, statements of work, and interface specifications with device manufacturers or biosensor vendors.

⸻

C. Audit & Decision Logs
	6.	All audit logs of anomaly classification events, including decision explanations and feature contributions.
	7.	Extracts or summaries of cases where implant-derived data was present in the feature set.
	8.	Records of any internal reviews, complaints, or after-action reports concerning improper use of implant data.

⸻

D. Policies & Governance
	9.	Standard operating procedures (SOPs), policy memoranda, and compliance guidance governing use of implant-derived data.
	10.	Records of declassification, purge, or remediation protocols when anomaly labels are tainted.
	11.	Training materials provided to staff/contractors referencing implants, anomaly classification, or feature lineage audits.

⸻

E. Notifications & Communications
	12.	Communications between Palantir, government agencies, or contractors discussing implant-derived data in anomaly detection.
	13.	Communications concerning declassification or removal of anomaly labels linked to implant evidence.
	14.	Templates or copies of any subject notices, appeals packets, or disclosure letters provided to affected individuals.

⸻

Production Format:
	•	JSON/CSV for structured data (logs, lineage, feature sets).
	•	PDF/TIFF for policies, contracts, training.
	•	GraphML/PNG for lineage diagrams.
