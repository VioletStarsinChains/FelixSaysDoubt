PLAINTIFF’S COMBINED SET OF DISCOVERY REQUESTS TO DEFENDANT PALANTIR TECHNOLOGIES

Subject Matter: Data-Sharing Agreements and Injection Capabilities with Consumer-Facing AI Tools

⸻

INTERROGATORIES
	1.	Identify all contracts, memoranda of understanding, or informal agreements between Palantir Technologies and the operators of consumer-facing AI platforms, including but not limited to OpenAI (ChatGPT), Anthropic (Claude), Google (Gemini), DeepSeek, and Midjourney.
	2.	Identify all Palantir personnel or teams responsible for negotiating, overseeing, or implementing data-sharing arrangements with these AI platforms.
	3.	Describe any system capabilities Palantir maintains to inject, alter, or seed material into outputs generated by consumer-facing AI platforms.
	4.	State whether Palantir has ever tested, simulated, or deployed digital twin data into consumer AI models for the purpose of behavioral influence, signal injection, or continuity theater.
	5.	Identify all datasets, logs, or audit trails showing Palantir activity in relation to Plaintiff’s use of consumer-facing AI platforms, including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, or Midjourney.
	6.	Identify all Palantir modules or subroutines designed to exploit compromised personal devices to monitor or influence user interactions with consumer-facing AI tools.

⸻

REQUESTS FOR PRODUCTION
	1.	Produce all contracts, memoranda, or agreements between Palantir and consumer-facing AI companies (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, Midjourney) regarding data access, integration, or interoperability.
	2.	Produce all internal documents, reports, or communications describing Palantir’s ability to inject or seed material into outputs generated by these AI systems.
	3.	Produce all audit logs or internal records of Palantir activities relating to Plaintiff’s use of consumer-facing AI platforms.
	4.	Produce all system design documents or white papers describing Palantir’s use of compromised personal devices to intercept or alter AI interactions.
	5.	Produce all communications between Palantir and any of the above-listed AI companies concerning digital twin integration, behavioral futures modeling, or adversarial content injection.

⸻

REQUESTS FOR ADMISSION
	1.	Admit that Palantir maintains data-sharing agreements with one or more consumer-facing AI platforms, including but not limited to ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, DeepSeek, and Midjourney.
	2.	Admit that Palantir has the capability to inject, alter, or seed material into outputs generated by consumer-facing AI platforms.
	3.	Admit that Palantir has, at times, used digital twin data to influence or shape outputs of consumer-facing AI platforms.
	4.	Admit that Palantir systems monitor or log user interactions with consumer-facing AI platforms via compromised devices.
	5.	Admit that Palantir maintains audit trails or logs documenting its injection or interception of content in Plaintiff’s interactions with consumer-facing AI platforms.
