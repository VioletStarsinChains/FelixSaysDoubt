INTERROGATORIES

Interrogatory No. 1: Identify all records, datasets, anomaly tags, or digital twin attributes maintained by Palantir referencing Plaintiff’s employment at:
	•	Nancy’s Hustle, 2704 Polk St A, Houston, TX 77003
	•	Food of Life Bakeshop, 2500 Marina Bay Dr Suite W, League City, TX 77573
	•	Jersey Bagels, 9522 Huffmeister Rd #500, Houston, TX 77095

Interrogatory No. 2: Identify all Palantir datasets, reports, or client-facing dashboards that included information about health code or food safety violations at these establishments.

Interrogatory No. 3: State whether Palantir has ever classified or flagged Plaintiff’s opposition to unsafe food safety practices as anomalous behavior.

Interrogatory No. 4: Identify all Palantir clients in the Greater Houston metropolitan area who received deliverables or dashboards that referenced Plaintiff’s employment or refusal to engage in unsafe food practices.

Interrogatory No. 5: Describe all Palantir datasets, deliverables, or analytic products that reference “coordinated attrition” in the Greater Houston metro between January 1, 2022 and May 1, 2025.

Interrogatory No. 6: Identify all Palantir personnel, contractors, or client accounts with access to datasets that referenced Plaintiff’s Houston-area employment or broader attrition strategies in the region.
