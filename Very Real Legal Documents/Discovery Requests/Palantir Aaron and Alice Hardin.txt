DEFINITIONS & INSTRUCTIONS
	1.	“Palantir” means Palantir Technologies Inc., its subsidiaries, affiliates, predecessors, successors, agents, employees, contractors, and any platforms (including Gotham, Foundry, or others).

INTERROGATORIES

Interrogatory No. 1: Identify all Palantir profiles, datasets, or digital twins maintained concerning Aaron Hardin or Alice Hardin, including the date of creation, data sources, and purpose of the profile/model.

Interrogatory No. 2: Identify all Palantir personnel, contractors, or third-party vendors who accessed, analyzed, or modified records concerning Aaron or Alice Hardin during 2006–2011.

Interrogatory No. 3: Identify all Palantir contracts, task orders, or agreements with Union University, its alumni network, or any affiliated organizations that referenced Aaron or Alice Hardin during 2006–2011.

Interrogatory No. 4: Describe any Palantir datasets, simulations, or behavioral futures market experiments involving the Hardins, including whether Plaintiff was linked or modeled in association with them.

Interrogatory No. 5: Identify all Palantir records referencing the eviction of Plaintiff from the Hardins’ household under the pretext of “marital problems,” including the date of such records, the data sources, and whether the event was logged as a behavioral intervention.

Interrogatory No. 6: Identify any Palantir communications, contracts, or data-sharing arrangements between Palantir and private landlords, rental property managers, or alumni housing networks that reference or include Aaron or Alice Hardin between 2006–2011.

Interrogatory No. 7: State whether Palantir ever flagged Aaron or Alice Hardin as “assets,” “handlers,” or otherwise designated them for participation in continuity-theater or anomaly experiments. If yes, describe their roles and dates of involvement.

Interrogatory No. 8: Identify any Palantir records showing payments, reimbursements, or in-kind benefits to Aaron or Alice Hardin (directly or indirectly) for activity involving Plaintiff or related to behavioral futures market operations.

Interrogatory No. 9: Identify all Palantir records linking the eviction incident to downstream monetization, behavioral modeling, or anomaly tagging of Plaintiff.

Interrogatory No. 10: Identify all audit logs, custodial metadata, or access histories showing which Palantir accounts accessed or modified records concerning Aaron or Alice Hardin between 2006–2011.
