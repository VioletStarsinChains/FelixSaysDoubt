SECTION A: DOCUMENT REQUESTS
	1.	Procurement Records
All purchase orders, invoices, contracts, or other procurement records from 2005–present relating to the acquisition of chemicals, pharmaceuticals, biologically active compounds, or treated coatings intended for use in desiccant packs, moisture absorbers, or packaging inserts.
	2.	Supplier & Subcontractor Communications
All communications (including emails, memoranda, letters, and meeting notes) with chemical, pharmaceutical, or biotechnology suppliers or subcontractors concerning the development, supply, or modification of desiccant packs or packaging inserts.
	3.	Safety & Regulatory Filings
All Safety Data Sheets (SDS/MSDS), toxicology assessments, or regulatory filings related to desiccant packs or packaging inserts sold or distributed by Uline.
	4.	Research & Development
All internal reports, testing data, or R&D documents referring to microencapsulation, slow-release, volatilization, or biologically active coatings in connection with desiccant packs or moisture absorbers.
	5.	Government Contracts
All contracts, requests for proposals (RFPs), or communications with federal agencies (including but not limited to DoD, FDA, GSA, DHS, and intelligence agencies) concerning desiccant packs, moisture absorbers, or packaging inserts, from 2005–present.
	6.	Marketing & Product Descriptions
All product descriptions, catalogs, marketing materials, or internal memoranda referring to “enhanced,” “performance,” “stabilized,” or “treated” desiccant packs or related packaging products.
	7.	Incident Reports & Complaints
All consumer complaints, incident reports, or internal investigations concerning unexpected odors, residues, health effects, or adverse reactions associated with desiccant packs supplied by Uline.

SECTION B: PHYSICAL OBJECT REQUESTS
	1.	Representative Samples
Provide sealed, unused desiccant packs, moisture absorbers, and packaging inserts (including silica gel, clay-based, molecular sieves, or other variants) manufactured, packaged, or distributed by Uline, from each year 2005 to present, with accompanying lot numbers and batch records.
	2.	Government Contract Fulfillment Samples

	4.	R&D and Pilot Program Samples
Provide any experimental or pilot desiccant packs produced internally or with subcontractors that involved coatings, microencapsulation, or active compound treatments.
