Plaintiff’s First Discovery Requests to Defendant Starbucks Corporation

Subject: Celebrity and VIP Visits, Tracking, and Orchestration

⸻

Interrogatories
	1.	Identify all policies, practices, or programs Starbucks maintains for tracking or recording celebrity or VIP visits to its retail stores.
	2.	Describe any communications protocols followed by employees when a celebrity, VIP, or other notable figure enters a Starbucks location.
	3.	State whether Starbucks coordinates or logs celebrity visits for marketing, public relations, or research purposes.
	4.	Identify all third parties, contractors, or agencies with whom Starbucks has shared data or records concerning celebrity or VIP visits.
	5.	Describe any occasions on which Starbucks has coordinated the presence of celebrities, university faculty, or other notable individuals in its retail locations as part of promotions, research, or observation programs.

⸻

Requests for Admission
	1.	Admit that Starbucks tracks or records celebrity or VIP visits to its retail stores.
	2.	Admit that Starbucks has shared data about celebrity or VIP visits with third parties.
	3.	Admit that Starbucks has coordinated the presence of celebrities or VIPs in stores for purposes beyond routine customer service.
	4.	Admit that Starbucks has used celebrity or VIP visits in connection with customer behavior research or marketing analysis.
	5.	Admit that Starbucks has not disclosed to customers or employees the existence of programs involving the orchestration or observation of celebrity encounters.

⸻

Requests for Production
	1.	All documents, logs, or databases tracking celebrity or VIP visits to Starbucks retail stores.
	2.	All communications between Starbucks employees, management, or contractors regarding the presence of celebrities, VIPs, or university faculty in retail locations.
	3.	All policies, training materials, or directives concerning employee handling of celebrity or VIP customers.
	4.	All contracts, agreements, or communications with third parties regarding the coordination or recording of celebrity or VIP visits to Starbucks stores.
	5.	All documents describing or analyzing the use of celebrity or VIP appearances in Starbucks retail locations for research, observation, or behavioral testing purposes.
