AFFIDAVIT OF KELLYN CLAY

I, Kellyn Clay, being duly sworn, depose and state as follows:
	1.	My name is Kellyn Clay. I am over the age of eighteen and competent to make this affidavit. The statements herein are based on my personal knowledge and recollection.
	2.	Shortly after the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020, I had an in-person conversation with a former partner who had previously served in the United States Air Force.
	3.	During this conversation, he told me that a woman he had dated following the end of our relationship had died as a result of alcohol abuse.
	4.	Soon after this disclosure, he relocated to Faribault, Minnesota.
	5.	After leaving the military, this individual became employed as a diesel mechanic for Caterpillar (CAT), working on heavy equipment.
	6.	His father worked as an electrician for 3M, a Minnesota-based multinational industrial and manufacturing corporation.
	7.	He had also disclosed to me in the past that his father was an alcoholic who had physically abused him during his childhood.
	8.	At the time of this conversation and reflecting on these details, I noted that alcohol, infrastructure, and utility-related employment had been recurring themes in various unusual or concerning circumstances I have experienced.
	9.	I make this affidavit to preserve the factual record of these conversations and circumstances as I directly recall them. I reserve the right to supplement this statement with additional details or corroborating information if they become available.
